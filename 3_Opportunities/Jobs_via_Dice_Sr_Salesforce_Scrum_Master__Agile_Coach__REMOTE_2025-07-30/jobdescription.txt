Title: Salesforce Scrum Master

Location: Remote

Target Start Date: 8/25/25

Type: Contract-to-hire

Pay: $65 - 75/hr

Job Description:

 We are seeking an experienced Sr. Scrum Master to lead and facilitate Agile delivery for two cross-functional Scrum teams-one focused on Salesforce CRM and the other on AWS/Java-based application development. The ideal candidate will bring strong Agile leadership, deep experience in the financial services industry, and a passion for continuous improvement. 
 The Senior Scrum Master will drive the execution of Agile practices, facilitate Scrum ceremonies, provide team coaching, and use JIRA to monitor and report on team metrics including burndown charts, velocity, and other KPIs. This role will also contribute to the refinement of our Agile processes and work closely with Product Owners, Engineering Managers, and stakeholders to ensure effective and transparent delivery. 

Responsibilities:

 Serve as Scrum Master for two Scrum teams (Salesforce CRM and AWS/Java development). 
 Facilitate all standard Scrum ceremonies including Daily Standups, Sprint Planning, Backlog Grooming, Sprint Reviews, Retrospectives, and System Demos. 
 Drive and update Agile best practices and coach team members on Scrum principles and frameworks. 
 Leverage JIRA to create and maintain Agile metrics, dashboards, burndown charts, sprint reports, and ensure data hygiene. 
 Collaborate with Product Owners to ensure well-defined product backlogs that are groomed and prioritized for upcoming sprints. 
 Identify and remove impediments to team progress; foster a collaborative, self-organizing, and accountable team culture. 
 Partner with stakeholders and leadership to promote Agile practices across the organization. 
 Conduct retrospectives and work with teams to implement continuous improvement initiatives. 
 Contribute to the evolution of Agile practices across teams, including templates, playbooks, and coaching tools. 
 Help manage interdependencies between teams and ensure alignment with product and technology roadmaps. 
 Champion Agile maturity and continuous learning across all teams. 

Required Skills:

 7+ years of experience as a Scrum Master, including supporting multiple Scrum teams concurrently. 
 Proven experience with Salesforce CRM and AWS / Java-based dev teams 
 Strong background in the financial services industry-preferably within fintech, banking, or capital markets. 
 Deep understanding and practical application of Agile principles and Scrum frameworks. 
 Proficiency in JIRA and Confluence, with experience configuring dashboards and generating Agile reports. 
 Ability to reinvigorate Agile practices on two development teams 
 Strong experience with reviewing and updating Agile processes 
 Strong facilitation, coaching, and communication skills with both technical and business stakeholders. 
 Demonstrated ability to drive Agile adoption, identify team anti-patterns, and implement improvements. 
 Familiarity with SAFe, Scrum of Scrums or other scaled Agile frameworks is a plus. 

Bonus Skills:

 Scrum Master certification (CSM, PSM II or above). 
 Agile Coach certification or equivalent experience in Agile transformation efforts. 
 Experience with Agile metrics beyond Jira (e.g., Jira Align, Advanced Roadmaps, or Agile analytics tools). 
 Experience working in a remote team environment.
