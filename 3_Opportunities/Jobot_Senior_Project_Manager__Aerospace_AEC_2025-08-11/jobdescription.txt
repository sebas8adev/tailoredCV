A Bit About Us

We are an employee-owned design and construction firm that specializes in highly technical and complex projects across industries such as aerospace, aviation, entertainment, and advanced manufacturing. With a collaborative and innovation-driven culture, the company has built a strong reputation for delivering specialized solutions in demanding environments.

The Project Manager is responsible for independently overseeing complex projects from start to finish, ensuring alignment with client expectations and the firm's mission. This role involves leading internal teams and external stakeholders, managing scope, schedule, budget, risk, and documentation, while also handling contracts, proposals, and project reporting. The position requires strong communication, leadership, and organizational skills developed through progressive experience and education.
