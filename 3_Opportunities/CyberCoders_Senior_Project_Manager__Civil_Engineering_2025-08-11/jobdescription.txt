About the job
Job Title: Senior Civil Engineer

Job Location: Orlando, FL

Salary: $130k-160k

Requirements: 8+ years of civil engineering and land development experience. PE License in Florida required.

Position Overview

We are seeking a highly experienced Senior Civil Engineer to lead and manage various infrastructure projects. The ideal candidate will have a strong background in land development and a proven track record in residential, commercial, and roadway projects. This role requires expertise in site grading, stormwater management, utility construction, and drainage solutions, with proficiency in AutoCAD.

Key Responsibilities

Lead and manage civil engineering projects from conception through completion.
Design and analyze land development plans for residential and commercial projects.
Coordinate with architects, contractors, and other stakeholders to ensure project success.
Conduct site grading and drainage design to meet regulatory requirements.
Oversee utility construction and ensure compliance with industry standards.
Prepare reports, specifications, and cost estimates for projects.
Utilize AutoCAD software to create detailed engineering drawings and plans.
Mentor junior engineers and provide guidance on technical issues.

Qualifications

Bachelor's degree in Civil Engineering or related field.
Professional Engineer (PE) license required.
8+ years of experience in civil engineering, with a focus on land development.
Strong knowledge of site grading, stormwater management, and utility construction.
Proficient in AutoCAD and other civil engineering software.
