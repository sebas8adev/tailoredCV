Job Title: Oracle Fusion Project Manager with ERP background
Location: Remote
Job Type: Contract
Visa - USC only 
Job Description:
This role focuses on leading and managing the implementation of Oracle Fusion ERP (Enterprise Resource Planning) solutions, typically from the initial planning stages through to post-go-live support. 
Key Responsibilities
Primary - Finance, HCM and SCM
Secondary - PPM, EPM and CX.
A Oracle Fusion Project Manager with an ERP background is responsible for:
Developing and executing project plans, managing budgets, and ensuring projects are delivered on time.
Serving as the main client contact, building relationships, and coordinating with stakeholders to define project scope and deliverables.
Leading and managing cross-functional teams, including developers and analysts, to achieve project goals.
Overseeing full-cycle Oracle ERP implementations or upgrades, providing guidance on Oracle Fusion ERP modules, and managing data migration and integration.
Managing testing phases (unit, integration, UAT), cutover planning, and post-production support.
Identifying and mitigating project risks, managing project scope, budget, timelines, and quality.
Communicating project progress to stakeholders through regular updates and reports.
Developing and implementing best practices, maintaining project documentation, and potentially participating in business development.
