Job Description:
About the job
***Must be able to work Pacific Time hours

We are seeking an experienced Senior Project Manager with strong healthcare payer expertise to lead clinical reporting initiatives within a Utilization Management (UM) environment. This role requires deep understanding of medical claims and Prior Authorization (PA) data, combined with strong Agile delivery leadership and cross-functional coordination skills.
The ideal candidate is comfortable operating independently, managing multiple workstreams, mitigating risks proactively, and driving alignment across business and technical stakeholders. This position requires availability to work Pacific Time (California hours).

Key Responsibilities:
Program & Project Leadership:
Build, manage, and maintain integrated project plans across multiple workstreams (clinical, reporting, data, engineering).
Lead end-to-end delivery of clinical reporting initiatives within Utilization Management.
Manage release planning, stakeholder communication cadence, and executive reporting.
Drive alignment between business priorities and technical execution, balancing scope, timeline, and quality tradeoffs.
Healthcare Domain Execution:
Apply strong knowledge of healthcare payer operations, including Utilization Management workflows.
Oversee initiatives involving medical claims data and Prior Authorization datasets.
Ensure accurate reporting logic, KPI alignment, and data integrity across clinical reporting solutions.
Agile & Scrum Delivery:
Independently facilitate Scrum ceremonies (Sprint Planning, Daily Standups, Reviews, Retrospectives).
Manage sprint cycles, backlog prioritization, and roadmap planning.
Partner with Product Owners and technical leads to ensure clear requirements and delivery transparency.
Risk & Governance:
Own RAID (Risks, Assumptions, Issues, Dependencies) management.
Proactively identify, track, and mitigate risks to maintain delivery timelines.
Communicate tradeoffs and impacts clearly to stakeholders.
Required Qualifications:
8+ years of project management experience within healthcare payer environments.
Proven experience supporting clinical reporting initiatives within Utilization Management (UM).
Strong understanding of:
Medical claims data structures
Prior Authorization workflows and reporting
Demonstrated experience building and managing integrated project plans across multiple teams.
Hands-on RAID management and risk mitigation expertise.
Experience independently leading Scrum ceremonies and managing Agile sprint cycles.
Strong stakeholder management and executive communication skills.
Ability to manage delivery tradeoffs between business and technical teams.
Must be able to work California (Pacific Time) business hours.
Preferred Qualifications:
PMP, PMI-ACP, or Scrum Master certification.
Experience working with data/analytics and reporting platforms (e.g., Power BI, Tableau, SQL-based reporting).
Experience in regulated healthcare environments.

Salary/Rate: $55-/$70/HR (depends on experience level). This is a contract position with candidates expected to work 40 hours/ week.
