About the job
Are you highly motivated, hard-working, and seeking to join a growth-focused consulting & engineering firm? Are you looking for a company that will invest in your development and grow your professional skills? If so, consider a career with Apex!

Powered by record sales, extraordinary client retention, strategic acquisitions, and an entrepreneurial spirit, Apex Companies is one of the fastest growing engineering and environmental consulting firms in the US. We were recently recognized by the Zweig Group as one of the fastest growing firms in the AEC industry. We take pride in providing our clients with exceptional service and have earned numerous awards for project excellence. We continue to grow, and we want you to grow with us.

We are seeking an experienced Senior Project Manager, Transportation with a background in the design and management of Transportation projects. The Project Manager should have 8+ years of experience in Transportation (Roadway) design and demonstrated effectiveness in planning and managing projects, maintaining budgets and schedules, and building and maintaining positive client relations. Applicants must be motivated with a desire for personal growth and advancement, as well as firm growth, and work well in a collaborative team atmosphere.

This is a remote position and can work anywhere withing the United States.

Responsibilities

 Supervise and direct the preparation of design and construction bid documents for Transportation (roadway) projects involving multi-modal accommodations (Complete Streets) including corridor, intersection, and downtown improvements
 Effectively present at meetings with clients and the public
 Maintain project financials/budgets, schedules, and project advancement
 Manage/mentor staff and provide guidance, manage workloads, and provide issue resolution
 Prepare project scopes and budgets for proposals and contracts
 Maintain communication with clients including municipalities and regional agencies

Minimum Requirements

What we're looking for:

Bachelors Degree in Civil or Transportation Engineering plus 10+ years of relevant experience (Roadway) including design and project management AutoCAD/Civil 3D
Possess a broad understanding of transportation design and planning standards, specifically standards and submission processes for state agencies
