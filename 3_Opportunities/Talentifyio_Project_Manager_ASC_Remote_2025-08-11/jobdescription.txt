Job Description:
About the job
Work Flexibility: Remote

Performs project management duties involving cross-functional teams focused on implementing cross-divisional ASC agreements. Manages projects from initiation through delivery by engaging with external customers, internal customers, Flex, and business unit partners. Organizes activities to facilitate the progression of a project, including interdepartmental meetings, proactive planning, and effective communication to key internal and external stakeholders.

Responsibilities

 Supports 4 ASC Regional Managers
 Communicate, coordinate, and align with internal partners for equipment delivery, installation, and training to assure for a seamless customer experience for new or expanding surgery centers.
 Coordinate across Stryker's internal supply chain to streamline logistics
 Highly responsive to internal sales teams and external customers
 Support co-marketing opportunities with strategic accounts
 Demonstrates an ability to lead without direct authority, communicate effectively, and influence both internal and external stakeholders
 Provides education virtually and/or in-person to the salesforce and customers regarding our implementation program
 Outlines and articulates relevant roles and responsibilities to enable utilization of key resources and adherence to established processes
 Attends essential in-person meetings and/or installations to consult and facilitate customer experience
 Executes processes utilizing exceptional organizational skills/attention to detail

Qualifications (Required)

B.A. or B.S. degree
4+ years of work experience required
2+ years medical device experience required
Customer-facing experience required

Qualifications (Preferred)

Cross-divisional experience within Stryker preferred
Marketing/supply chain /project management experience within Medical Device and/or clinical experience within a hospital setting preferred

This is a Remote role and can sit anywhere in the US
