About the job
JOB SUMMARY
The Project Manager is responsible for overseeing software and IT-related projects from initiation to completion. The role will collaborate with cross-functional teams, including software developers, product managers, and customer success to define project goals, establish timelines, and manage deliverables. This role requires strong technical project management skills, attention to detail, and the ability to navigate a dynamic, fast-paced environment.

RESPONSIBILITIES
Plan, manage, and execute IT and software development projects from inception to completion
Define project scope, objectives, and success metrics in collaboration with key stakeholders
Develop and maintain project timelines, budgets, and risk mitigation plans
Coordinate internal resources and third parties, clients and vendors for the outstanding execution of multiple projects 
Work closely with software engineers, product managers, clients and customer success team to ensure alignment and clear communication
Manage Agile and Scrum processes, including sprint planning, backlog grooming, and retrospectives
Identify and resolve project risks, blockers, and dependencies
Ensure projects meet quality standards, security requirements, and compliance guidelines
Provide regular updates and reports on project status to leadership and stakeholders
Continuously improve project management processes to enhance efficiency and effectiveness

REQUIREMENTS 
Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Information Technology, Business, or a related field (or equivalent experience).
5+ years of project management experience in IT or software development, preferably in a SaaS or software development environment.
Experience with Agile, Scrum, or other project management methodologies. PMP, PMI-ACP, or CSM certification.
Familiarity with software development lifecycles (SDLC), DevOps practices, cloud platforms like AWS, Azure, or Google Cloud, and IT infrastructure.
Proficiency in project management tools such as Jira, Asana, or other related platforms, strong ability to lead cross-functional teams, drive alignment, and communicate effectively with technical and non-technical stakeholders, ability to navigate challenges, manage competing priorities, and drive results in a fast-paced environment.
