About the job
Are you a service-minded problem solver? Do you want to put your leadership and communication skills to use to ensure our projects deliver the utmost value for our customers and their patients? We are currently looking for a Project Manager to work as a part of our Upgrades team.

As a Project Manager on our Upgrades team, you will have the opportunity to learn about Sectra's products, processes, and customers. You will have the opportunity to gain hands on experience with our product upgrades, early visibility into the latest and greatest versions of our technology, work across multiple different departments and teams, as well as interact with our global product developers. By seeing the organization from this perspective, you will build a stronger network and have a better understanding of how the Upgrades process works to better serve our customers. This approach not only exposes you to our technology operations, but also helps you build an understanding of our business - knowledge that is invaluable for a successful career at Sectra. You will get to work alongside our Upgrades Team to ensure that all projects are met on time and efficiently. You will be key in providing annual upgrades of our products to our customers and you will work closely with our Engineers, Application Specialists, and fellow Project Managers.

As a Project Manager on our Upgrades team, you will have the opportunity to:

Project manage simultaneous upgrade projects from initial kick off through post go live issue resolution
Plan, schedule, and document upgrade activities and outcomes from project meetings
Work with customers, upgrade team members, and account team members to successfully complete upgrade projects
Identifying, tracking, and escalating project and product issues/concerns
Provide weekly reports on project and issue status to management
Travel as required (less than 25%)
