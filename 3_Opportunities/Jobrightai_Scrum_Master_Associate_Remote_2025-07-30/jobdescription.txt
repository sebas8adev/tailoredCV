Job Summary:
Tekton Labs is a fast-growing Salesforce consulting firm helping enterprises and nonprofits unlock the full value of their CRM, AI, and cloud platforms. They are seeking a Scrum Master to join a high-performing team, enabling the success of Salesforce-related initiatives by championing agile practices and coaching teams through continuous improvement cycles.

Responsibilities:
• Guide 2–3 cross-functional teams through agile ceremonies and delivery planning.
• Facilitate daily stand-ups, sprint planning, retrospectives, and backlog refinement.
• Identify and resolve impediments to ensure timely delivery of product increments.
• Support the team’s growth in agile maturity, promoting best practices and adaptive frameworks (Scrum, Kanban, SAFe).
• Collaborate with Product Owners and Technical Leads to align delivery with business value.
• Champion a continuous improvement mindset across teams and stakeholders.
• Help teams navigate compliance-heavy environments where documentation and process rigor are essential.
• Act as a delivery enabler between stakeholders in regulated and unregulated business segments.
• Support the flow of work across Salesforce-related CRM initiatives (no specific cloud required).

Qualifications:

Required:
• 4+ years of experience as a Scrum Master or Agile Delivery Lead in enterprise environments.
• Hands-on experience facilitating agile ceremonies and managing blockers for multiple squads.
• Practical knowledge of agile frameworks (Scrum, Kanban); familiarity with SAFe is a plus.
• Proven ability to lead delivery cycles for CRM or Salesforce-based projects (cloud agnostic).
• Experience working in regulated industries (e.g., energy, finance, telecom, healthcare).
• Strong collaboration skills with Product Owners, technical teams, and business units.
• Fluent or upper-intermediate English for daily team communication and facilitation.

Preferred:
• Experience supporting product teams managing CRM ecosystems.
• Background in helping organizations shift from IT-led to business-led agile models.
• Scrum Master certification (CSM, PSM, or equivalent).
• Experience in the energy or utilities sector.

Company:
Tekton is a Technology Consultancy and Software Development company that builds products to improve people’s lives. Founded in 2008, the company is headquartered in Mountain View, California, USA, with a team of 51-200 employees. The company is currently Growth Stage.
