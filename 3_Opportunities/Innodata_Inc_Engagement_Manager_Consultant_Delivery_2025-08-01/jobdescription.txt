About The Role

We are seeking a consultative and delivery-focused Engagement Manager to serve as the primary interface between Innodata and our enterprise clients. This individual will be responsible for overseeing complex AI/ML data and content transformation engagements, ensuring delivery excellence while fostering long-term partnerships.

This is a high-impact role ideal for someone who thrives at the intersection of client management, solution execution, and operational excellence.

Key Responsibilities

Serve as the main point of contact for assigned enterprise client engagements
Build and maintain trusted advisor relationships with key client stakeholders
Understand client objectives and design delivery plans to align with their business goals
Lead end-to-end project execution, from kickoff through final delivery
Coordinate cross-functional teams across global delivery hubs
Manage delivery workflows related to AI data preparation, annotation, enrichment, and digital content transformation
Define and monitor KPIs, SLAs, and delivery milestones
Identify and address delivery risks, issues, and blockers proactively
Ensure accurate and timely communication with clients regarding progress, changes, and expectations
Track and manage scope, timeline, and budget across multiple workstreams
Maintain comprehensive documentation for all projects and client interactions
Collaborate with Sales, Solutions, Product, and Operations to align internal capabilities with client needs
Drive continuous improvement in delivery processes and service models
Provide strategic input on how clients can optimize AI workflows or digital operations
Translate client feedback into actionable insights for internal teams
Contribute to the development of new service offerings based on evolving client demands


Required Skills And Experience

5+ years of client delivery or program management experience in professional services, consulting, digital transformation, or AI/data services
Experience managing enterprise-level clients and complex delivery engagements
Strong project management skills with familiarity in Agile, Scrum, or Waterfall methodologies
Excellent verbal and written communication skills, with the ability to present to executive-level stakeholders
Ability to manage multiple projects simultaneously in a fast-paced environment
Experience leading geographically distributed teams
Understanding of AI/ML data workflows, digital content processing, or knowledge management
