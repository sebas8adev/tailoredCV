About the job
Senior Bridge Project Manager - Transportation & Structural Design (Florida)
Location: Orlando | Full-Time | Hybrid/Remote Flexibility
A leading engineering consulting firm is seeking a Senior Bridge Project Manager Manager to lead and expand its Transportation and Structural Engineering practice in Florida. This is a high-impact leadership role with a well-established and rapidly growing organization that offers exciting opportunities for career advancement and professional growth.
The firm:
-Annual bonuses, bi-annual salary reviews, and competitive salaries with one-time bonuses for new work won
-BIG on culture and rewarding employees
-Very open about supporting employees and their career track
-Multi-disciplinary national firm with ~900 people
-Split between municipal and FDOT work with a 40/60 split 
The ideal candidate will bring 10+ years of experience in structural design for transportation infrastructure, particularly with Florida Department of Transportation (FDOT) and other local agencies. This role combines technical leadership, project management, business development, and team mentorship.

Key Responsibilities

Provide leadership and mentorship to a multidisciplinary team of engineers, EITs, and CAD professionals.
Oversee the design and technical execution of bridge and transportation infrastructure projects.
Coordinate with internal and external stakeholders across disciplines.
Manage project budgets, schedules, and quality assurance processes.
Stay current with regulatory changes and agency requirements.
Lead proposal development, scope definition, and resource planning.
Participate in client meetings and conduct site visits (up to 25% travel).

Qualifications

Bachelor's degree in Civil Engineering (Master's preferred).
Licensed Professional Engineer (PE) in Florida.
Extensive experience with FDOT, Florida's Turnpike Enterprise, and CFX.
Design-Build project experience is a plus.
Strong communication, leadership, and organizational skills.
Proven ability to manage multiple priorities and deliver high-quality results.

What's Offered

Competitive salary aligned with market standards.
Comprehensive benefits package including medical, dental, vision, and disability coverage.
Paid parental leave and adoption assistance.
Generous PTO and paid holidays.
401(k) with company match.
Tuition reimbursement and professional development support.
A collaborative, supportive work environment with opportunities for advancement.
Join a team where your expertise will shape the future of transportation infrastructure in Florida. If you're ready to take the next step in your career and lead impactful projects, we'd love to hear from you.
