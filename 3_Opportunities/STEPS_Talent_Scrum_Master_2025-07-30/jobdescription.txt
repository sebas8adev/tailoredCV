Senior Scrum Master – Security Program
Location: 100% Remote (U.S. Based)
Start Date: Targeting Start by End of August
Type: Contract (12+ Months)

About the Role:
We are actively seeking a Senior Scrum Master / Agile Delivery Lead to support a high-impact Security Program across the enterprise. In this role, you will lead multiple scrum teams in applying Scaled Agile (SAFe) practices, while also driving team efficiency through advanced Agile metrics and reporting. This is a high-visibility role with a focus on Agile delivery maturity, cross-team collaboration, and measurable performance improvement.

Key Responsibilities:
Lead all Scrum ceremonies (Daily Standups, Sprint Planning, Reviews, Retrospectives, PI Planning).
Support 3–5 concurrent Scrum teams across the Security domain.
Act as a delivery enabler, removing impediments and advocating for team needs during PI Planning.
Track and manage Agile metrics using Jira and Confluence (e.g., Sprint Velocity, Capacity Planning, Rolled Stories, Burnup/Burndown).
Generate reports on team trends, sprint point variances, and resource forecasting needs.
Coach Product Owners in backlog creation and roadmap planning.
Collaborate with leadership to deliver product outcomes using SAFe frameworks.

Required Skills & Experience:
5+ years of experience as a Scrum Master or Scrum Lead.
Strong command of Scaled Agile (SAFe) and Agile-at-scale practices.
Proficient in Jira, Confluence, and Agile Program Boards.
Proven ability to manage Agile metrics and performance reporting across multiple teams.
Experience managing Security, Cloud, or other mission-critical programs.
Strong interpersonal and conflict resolution skills; ability to lead difficult conversations and protect team throughput.

Nice-to-Have:
Experience in the Financial Services industry
Background with AWS Cloud Delivery
SAFe Scrum Master (SSM) or Certified Scrum Master (CSM)
