Job Summary

The Clinical Project Manager reports directly to the Chief Services Officer. The Clinical Project Manager leads project teams comprised of JTG consultants and client staff. The Clinical Project Manager oversees and manages client projects, establishing goals and timelines for the project and ensuring that the goals are met.

Job Duties And Responsibilities

Lead and motivate project team
Manage and work collaboratively with team members, and both internal and external stakeholders
Implement and follow project governance protocols
Develop and maintain realistic and reasonable project timelines and task durations
Keep timelines on track by knowing when to escalate threats to the critical path
Monitor project progress while being able to see the big picture and not neglect the details
Conduct continual assessments and review feasibility analyses for project success and allocated resources
Estimate time and financial commitments required to complete project tasks 
Steer client and users through established change control processes
Be the owner of the project scope, address and mitigate scope creep
Build project plan documentation/reports and maintain for regular leadership review/updates
Implement and optimize process improvement where inefficiencies are observed
Collect, analyze and compose approved metrics set out to demonstrate project success/progress into presentations and reports
Document lessons learned activities for project closeout and evaluation
Manage transition support to operations team post go-live
Work collaboratively with clients to plan, install, implement and maintain hardware and software solutions
Abides by HIPAA regulations of confidentiality for all activities and information accessed
Determine project priorities based on needs of our Clients. Ensure completion of projects in a timely manner to comply with established deadlines. Actively work with Chief Services Officer and clients to determine and or adjust project priorities
Develop and maintain good relationships with clients and clients’ staff
Other tasks and projects as assigned by the CEO, or the Chief Services Officer
