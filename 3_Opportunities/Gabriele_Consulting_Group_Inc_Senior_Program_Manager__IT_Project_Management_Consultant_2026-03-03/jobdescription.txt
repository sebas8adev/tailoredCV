Position Summary:

We are seeking a skilled program manager with IT program and project management experience to serve as the critical link between business managers and technical teams. This role does not require deep technical expertise (such as coding), but it does require the ability to understand the intersection between interrelated tech applications and business goals, exceptional organizational skills, clear communication, and the ability to keep projects on track in a complex environment with multiple stakeholders.

The successful candidate will be adept at translating business requirements into actionable project plans, ensuring all parties are aligned on goals, timelines, and deliverables, and proactively addressing roadblocks to maintain project momentum.

As examples of the types of projects this position could work on, here are several projects we have been or are currently involved in with our current client:

Project management of very large unstructured data governance initiative, including managing unstructured data separation strategy and execution for a large-scale spinoff
Project management of a large-scale migration from prior versions of SAP and JDE ERPs to SAP S/4 HANA.
Program management of the ingestion of data into the company’s Common Data Layer (CDL), which is a massive data repository, housing data from hundreds of apps and tens of thousands of tables.

Why Join Us

At GCG, you’ll have the opportunity to experience the best of both worlds—the agility and camaraderie of a small, collaborative team combined with the resources, scale, and challenges of working with a Fortune 100 company. You’ll be trusted with the autonomy to move projects forward and make an impact.

We offer flexible hours and remote work to support a healthy work–life balance, while giving you the chance to sharpen your project management and stakeholder communication skills in a fast-paced, high-visibility environment. Every day brings fresh variety, meaningful challenges, and the satisfaction of seeing your work influence high-profile IT initiatives that matter.

Key Responsibilities:

Act as the liaison between business stakeholders and technical teams to ensure clear, shared understanding of goals, priorities, and deliverables.
Develop and maintain detailed project plans, schedules, and progress reports.
Coordinate meetings, document action items, and track follow-up to ensure commitments are met.
Manage dependencies, risks, and issues, escalating when needed to keep work on track.
Facilitate effective communication across business and technical teams, ensuring information flows accurately and on time.
Monitor project progress against agreed timelines and adjust plans as needed.
Ensure all stakeholders are informed of status, upcoming milestones, and potential impacts to timelines or scope.
Support change management processes to ensure smooth implementation of new systems or processes.
