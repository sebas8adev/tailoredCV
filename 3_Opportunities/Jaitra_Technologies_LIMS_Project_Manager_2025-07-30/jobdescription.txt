About the job
Due to the nature of our clients and data compliance regulations, this position is open to
U.S. Citizens only that can pass DHS background investigation residing within the United States.

Location: Remote (U.S. Based)
Clearance: U.S. Citizenship + Ability to pass DHS Background Check
Employment Type: Full-Time

We are seeking a Technical Project Manager with hands-on experience leading LabVantage LIMS implementations in highly regulated public sector environments. This role will lead the full lifecycle of LIMS projects for a major federal client (Department of Homeland Security), acting as the key liaison between scientific stakeholders, technical teams, and government leadership.
If you're a proactive project leader who thrives in mission-critical environments and understands the complexities of LIMS delivery, we’d love to hear from you.

What You’ll Do

Lead LabVantage LIMS implementation from requirements through go-live
Coordinate across cross-functional teams (architects, developers, QA, and client stakeholders)
Own project plans, financial tracking, risk management, and status reporting
Translate technical deliverables into clear, actionable updates for non-technical stakeholders
Oversee documentation, validation (IQ/OQ/PQ), and ensure compliance with NIST, ATO, and DHS governance
Facilitate project reviews, team standups, and executive briefings
Support contract oversight with internal and external stakeholders
Mentor internal teams and support pre-sales/project briefings when needed

What We’re Looking For
Bachelor’s degree in Life Sciences, Information Systems, Informatics, or related field
5+ years managing complex LIMS projects (LabVantage preferred)
Experience working with federal/public sector clients (DHS, NIH, FDA, DoD, etc.)
Understanding of NIST 800, GxP, Part 11, and data integrity standards
Proven ability to manage teams and client communication
Familiarity with Agile/Kanban delivery
PMP certification preferred

Bonus Points
Background in lab science or lab informatics configuration
Experience in DHS environments or federal contracting (FAR/DFARS)
