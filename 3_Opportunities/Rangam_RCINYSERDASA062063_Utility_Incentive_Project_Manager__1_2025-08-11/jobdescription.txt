Project Overview:
This Project Manager will support the rollout of the Inflation Reduction Act (IRA) Retail Point-of-Sale rebate program launched in October 2024. The program includes collaboration with retailers, call center support, contract management, and data reporting responsibilities.

Key Responsibilities:
Lead the planning, execution, and reporting for the IRA Retail Point-of-Sale rebate program.
Collaborate with retailers, contractors, and implementation partners to manage program applications, onboarding, and enrollment.
Own program operations including budget tracking, issue resolution, and stakeholder communication.
Serve as first line of support (before full call center implementation) to answer retailer and contractor queries via email or phone.
Ensure compliance with DOE requirements and utility rebate policies.
Implement and oversee data reporting processes using Salesforce and internal tools.
Analyze sales, rebate submissions, and compliance data to identify trends, risks, and areas for improvement.
Support deployment of future rebate expansions (e.g., induction, cooling appliances).
Coordinate with Codes, Products & Standards team and track project milestones using tools like Asana, SharePoint, and MS Teams.
