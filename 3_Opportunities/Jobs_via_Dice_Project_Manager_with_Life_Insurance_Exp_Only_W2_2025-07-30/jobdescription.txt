 (Only W2)

Job Overview:

TEKsystems is seeking a Senior Project Manager Consultant with strong experience in the life insurance industry, specifically in evaluating and selecting modern core insurance platforms. This consultant will partner with a small but growing life insurance company to lead and manage the platform evaluation and selection process. The ideal candidate has led similar initiatives, facilitated vendor engagement, and worked cross-functionally to guide organizations through major core system decisions.

Key Responsibilities:

Lead the end-to-end evaluation and selection process for a life insurance core platform
Develop and manage project timelines, milestones, and deliverables related to platform selection
Coordinate stakeholder input, gather business and technical requirements, and align teams
Facilitate RFI/RFP processes, organize vendor demos, and manage communication with platform providers
Create and present comparative analysis, business cases, and recommendations to executive leadership
Drive effective communication between business, IT, and vendor partners throughout the engagement
Support early-stage planning for implementation readiness and transition

Required Experience:

8+ years of project management experience within the life insurance or insurance technology space
Proven success leading platform evaluation or transformation initiatives for life insurance carriers
Deep knowledge of modern life insurance platforms, including:
LIDP Titanium, Socotra, FAST, Sapiens CoreSuite, LifePro, OIPA (Oracle Insurance), ALIP, DXC Assure, TCS BaNCS
Experience working with small to mid-sized insurance companies is highly preferred
Strong leadership, communication, presentation, and documentation skills

Preferred Skills:

PMP or similar project management certification
Experience working in both Agile and Waterfall environments
Ability to translate business needs into technical criteria during vendor evaluations
