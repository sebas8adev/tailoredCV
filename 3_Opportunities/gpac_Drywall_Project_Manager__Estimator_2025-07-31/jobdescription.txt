About the job
Drywall Estimator 

Build your future with a company that values accuracy, trust, and teamwork.

Are you a skilled Drywall Estimator looking for your next career move? Join a well-established and growing drywall contractor with a reputation for high-quality work and a strong team culture. This is a great opportunity to be a key part of a laid-back but driven team where your expertise truly makes a difference.

What You'll Do

Estimating & Bidding

Prepare accurate takeoffs and estimates using On-Screen Takeoff (OST), QuickBid
Analyze blueprints and specs to create competitive bids
Account for labor, materials, equipment, and overhead
Collaborate with internal teams to align estimates with client needs and timelines

Project Support

Work closely with Project Managers to transition successful bids into active jobs
Participate in preconstruction meetings and cost planning
Assist with value engineering and project phasing plans as needed

Collaboration & Communication

Coordinate with clients, GCs, subcontractors, and vendors
Maintain clear documentation of bids, revisions, and awarded jobs
Represent the company professionally in all pre-award interactions

What You'll Need

2-3 years of drywall estimating experience
Familiarity with OST, QuickBid, and Microsoft Office
Experience with structural framing and exterior systems
Project experience with hospitals, casinos, hotels, and schools
Average project size: $1M-$19M
