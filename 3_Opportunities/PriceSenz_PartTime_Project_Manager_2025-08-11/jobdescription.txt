About the job
Job Title: Part-Time Project Manager
Work Type: Contract – Part-Time (5–10 hours/week)
Location: 100% Remote (must reside and work within the United States)

Job Description: We are seeking a highly experienced Project Manager to support ongoing initiatives on a part-time, remote basis. The selected individual will perform advanced-level project management work with considerable autonomy, collaborating with a wide range of stakeholders, including senior leadership across the agency. This role requires solid experience in Agile project frameworks and enterprise content management systems.

Responsibilities:
Lead and manage multiple concurrent projects with complex integrations and diverse stakeholders.
Work closely with technical and business teams to deliver projects on time and within scope.
Plan, track, and report project progress, risks, and milestones to leadership.
Support implementation and enhancements of enterprise content management systems.
Ensure alignment with TEA’s project management standards and procedures.
Communicate clearly and effectively with cross-functional teams and leadership.

Required Qualifications:
6+ years of experience managing complex projects with multiple stakeholders and systems integration.
6+ years of experience leading projects involving enterprise content management (ECM) solutions.
Strong familiarity with Agile methodologies and project management best practices.
Excellent written and verbal communication skills.
Ability to work independently with limited supervision.

Preferred Qualifications:
3+ years of experience working with Texas Education Agency or another state agency.
Prior experience with TEA’s standard project practices and procedures.
Hands-on experience managing projects using Alfresco ECM, including enhancement and maintenance work.
