Job Description:
About the job
Presidio, Where Teamwork and Innovation Shape the Future

At Presidio, we’re at the forefront of a global technology revolution, transforming industries through cutting-edge digital solutions and next-generation AI. We empower businesses—and their customers—to achieve more through innovation, automation, and intelligent insights.

The Role

As a Senior Project Manager you will lead all aspects of IT projects including project scope, schedule, resources, quality, costs, and change control. Presidio PMs work directly with the project team, clients and partners to lead and ensure successful project delivery within scope, on time, and within budget, and are expected to be able to communicate effectively across all levels of the organization and project teams.

Travel Requirements

This position does not require any travel.

Job Responsibilities

The Project Manager will be a key piece of the Project Management Office for the Cloud Services division of Presidio. 
This team manages and drives our Cloud Services most critical, complex, and enterprise-level projects for our clients in the Healthcare, Financial Services, Retail, Technology, and Energy industries. 
The Project Manager will deliver professional service projects in the Cloud Infrastructure and DevOps spaces, requiring a deep understanding of the Application Stack, Agile processes, technical project delivery, and Cloud (Amazon Web Services, Azure, and GCP). 
Drive end-to-end accountability for complex cloud initiatives—from initiation through closure—ensuring outcomes are met on time, within budget, and aligned to client business objectives. 
Serve as a critical liaison between technical teams, client stakeholders, business development, and solutions architecture—helping ensure smooth execution and strategic alignment across all parties. 
Acts as a consultative leader—translating technical execution into business outcomes, influencing across cross-functional teams without direct authority, and fostering trusted advisor relationships with clients. 
Serve as a strategic advisor to clients by translating technical delivery into meaningful business outcomes and decision-ready information. 
Facilitate executive-level client meetings, QBRs, and project status reviews with clarity, credibility, and responsiveness. 
The Project Manager supports a large Cloud and DevOps team delivering automation, assessments, cloud builds, application migrations, and technical workshops. 
Identify delivery gaps, propose process improvements, and drive standardization to scale project delivery across multiple engagements. 
Responsible for proactively identifying risks, dependencies, and process improvements to drive consistent, scalable delivery within dynamic and fast-paced environments. 
Manage change control and project scope rigorously to prevent scope creep and maintain delivery integrity. 
Act as a mentor and escalation point for junior PMs or coordinators on the team. 
Share best practices and lessons learned across the PMO and contribute to the maturity of the practice. 

Required Skills

Demonstrable skills across project management standard methodologies (Project Management Institute defined), Agile, SCRUM, Kanban, Waterfall).
Ability to manage Agile/Scrum/Kanban/Waterfall (Depending on client environment) processes to effectively deliver new applications and app modernization projects.
Proficient in Confluence, Jira, and Microsoft Project for project goal and timeline tracking.
Consultative mindset with a focus on client outcomes—able to align project execution with business goals, influence without authority, and foster trusted partnerships across technical and non-technical stakeholders.
Familiarity with Azure DevOps, Confluence, Trello, Smartsheet, and similar project management toolsets to manage project tracking, key performance indicators, project budget, resources, and timelines. 
Experience working with the Microsoft Office suite of tools (Outlook, Excel, Word, PowerPoint, and SharePoint).

Strong time management, analytical, communication, thought leadership, team building, motivational and customer service skills.
Excellent client-facing communication and relationship building skills.
Ability to learn quickly, adapt to change and have ownership of staying up-to-date on current and emerging technologies and tools as they relate to our business and project needs.
Passion, creativity, energy, and enthusiasm towards collaborating on the exciting growth and development of our company.
