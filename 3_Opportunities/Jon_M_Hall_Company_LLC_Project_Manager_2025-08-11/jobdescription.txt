Job Description:
About the job
Lead. Build. Deliver.

At Jon M. Hall Company, we don’t just construct infrastructure — we create lasting impact. We’re looking for an experienced and driven Project Manager to lead the planning, execution, and delivery of large-scale site development and utility projects. If you’re ready to take ownership, inspire teams, and deliver excellence, this is your opportunity to make a mark in a company that values precision, safety, and teamwork.

Key Responsibilities Pre-Construction Leadership

Review estimator information to assemble comprehensive scopes of work. 
Provide detailed job schedules, work orders, and budgets to the office and field teams before kickoff. 
Collaborate with field supervisors to review production goals, schedules, and budgets before work begins. 
Present weekly progress reports at operations meetings to track milestones and address challenges. 

Contract & Change Management

Oversee all changes to the project — including change orders, submittals, revised plan sets, and work order adjustments. 
Ensure timely documentation and owner approvals prior to work execution. 

Financial Oversight

Prepare accurate and timely pay requests for review by leadership. 
Participate in monthly financial review meetings, analyzing WIP reports, cost variances, and profitability for each project. 

Client & Stakeholder Communication

Serve as the primary liaison between owners, engineers, and company personnel. 
Build strong relationships with clients, ensuring open communication and trust throughout the project lifecycle. 

Project Closeout

Finalize jobs by managing retainage requests, punch lists, and as-built documentation. 

Collaboration with Estimating

Partner with estimators during the bid process, reviewing and signing off on proposals prior to submission. 

Company-Wide Standards

As a leader at Jon M. Hall Company, you will:

Evaluate, recommend, and implement improved construction methods and processes. 
Train, coach, and support teams to enhance productivity. 
Conduct all interactions professionally and respectfully. 
Notify leadership promptly of potential delays or issues. 
Maintain confidentiality of all proprietary information. 
Uphold all administrative and safety procedures in line with our company handbook and safety manual. 

What You Bring to the Table

Proven experience in project management within site development, earthwork, and underground utilities. 
Strong skills in budget control, scheduling, and team leadership. 
Exceptional communication and problem-solving abilities. 
Ability to balance multiple priorities while ensuring projects are delivered on time and on budget.
