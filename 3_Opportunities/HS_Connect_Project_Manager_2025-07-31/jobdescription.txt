Job Description:
About the job
Job Title: Project/Program Manager – HS Connect
Location: Remote (US Only
Employment Type: Full-Time (Salaried)

About HS Connect:
HS Connect is the leading patient advocacy organization for individuals living with Hidradenitis Suppurativa (HS). We are committed to creating resources, programs, and support systems that improve the quality of life for those affected by HS. Our mission is to serve the HS community while partnering with sponsors and industry leaders to build meaningful initiatives that drive awareness, education, and access to care.

Position Overview:
We are seeking a dynamic, entrepreneurial Project/Program Manager to lead the planning, launch, and execution of new initiatives as we secure funding for them. The ideal candidate is a self-starter who thrives in fast-paced, scrappy environments and can bring big-picture vision while managing details with precision. You will oversee multiple projects from concept to completion, ensuring they align with HS Connect’s mission and the needs of both the HS community and our sponsors.

Key Responsibilities:
Develop, launch, and oversee new programs as funding is secured, ensuring they are mission-driven and impact-focused.

Collaborate with leadership and sponsors to set program goals, deliverables, and success metrics.

Manage timelines, budgets, and resources across multiple projects simultaneously.Maintain strong communication with internal team members and external partners.

Identify gaps and propose creative, agile solutions to challenges that arise during project execution.

Prepare reports and updates for leadership and sponsors, highlighting progress and outcomes.

Work cross-functionally to ensure that all initiatives reflect HS Connect’s values and serve our community effectively.

Represent HS Connect in partner meetings, presentations, and industry events as needed.

Qualifications:
3+ years of experience in project management, program management, or operations—preferably in the nonprofit, healthcare, wellness, or medical sector.

Strong organizational skills with proven ability to manage multiple initiatives simultaneously.

Exceptional communication skills—both written and verbal—with the ability to engage and inspire diverse stakeholders.

Entrepreneurial mindset and a proactive problem solver with the ability to think strategically and execute tactically.

Self starter, you don’t wait to be asked for things, and you see each detail through across departments and follow up with peers.

Comfort working in small, agile teams where roles are fluid and collaboration is essential.

Proficiency in project management tools (e.g., Slack) and G Suite.

Familiarity with hidradenitis suppurativa, chronic illness advocacy, or healthcare projects is a major plus.

Preferred Skills:
Experience with sponsor or stakeholder management.

Background in medical, health, or wellness fields.

Grant management or fundraising experience.

Compensation:
HS Connect offers a flexible, remote-friendly work environment and opportunities to shape impactful programs that directly support the HS community. $75-85K depending on experience.
