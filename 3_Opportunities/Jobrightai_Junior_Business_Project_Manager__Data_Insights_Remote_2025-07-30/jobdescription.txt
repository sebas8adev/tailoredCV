Job Summary:
Redtech is helping their client with a Contractor search for a Business Project Manager in Data Insights. The role involves providing Business Project Management support to maintain business operations, manage budgets, and deliver actionable insights through reporting and analytics, particularly focusing on Azure cloud sales.

Responsibilities:
• Ensure consistent and accurate reporting of partner metrics.
• Support fiscal planning and forecasting activities.
• Maintain organizational tools and dashboards for business visibility.
• Provide administrative and analytical support to the Chief of Staff (COS).
• Monitor and report on Standard Partner Consumption Metrics.
• Track and analyze Partner Pipeline Metrics.
• Deliver Targeted Metric Reporting across Azure cloud solution areas and geographies.
• Manage Reporting Projects outside the standard rhythm of business.
• Provide Business Analytics and offer actionable insights.
• Oversee Budget Management using advanced Excel techniques.
• Create and maintain Power BI Dashboards.
• Maintain organizational knowledge bases including SharePoint and internal databases.
• Facilitate Administrative Tasks as required by the COS.
• Support Planning and Forecasting of fiscal activities.
