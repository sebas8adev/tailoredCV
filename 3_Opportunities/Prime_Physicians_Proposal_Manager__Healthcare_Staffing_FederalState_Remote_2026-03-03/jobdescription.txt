About the job
Job Title - Proposal Manager – Healthcare Staffing (Federal/State) (Remote)
Location: Remote (U.S.-based)
Start Date : April 15, 2026 or later

About Prime Physicians
Prime Physicians is a healthcare staffing and professional services firm supporting federal and state clients. Prime Physicians is seeking a Proposal Manager – Healthcare Staffing to lead end-to-end proposal development for competitive solicitations across federal and state markets, with an emphasis on healthcare staffing opportunities supporting IHS, VA, and/or DHA. This role is responsible for managing the full proposal lifecycle—planning, coordination, writing, compliance, reviews, and final production—while leading and directing a cross-functional proposal team.
This is a remote position and requires a candidate with deep domain expertise in healthcare staffing proposals and strong proposal team leadership.

Key Responsibilities
Lead full proposal lifecycle management from pre-RFP planning through submission (capture support, outlines, schedules, compliance matrices, writing plans, review cycles, and production).
Manage proposal teams (writers, SMEs, recruiters, operations, pricing, subcontractor/partner contacts) to deliver compliant, high-quality proposals on time.
Develop and maintain proposal schedules, milestones, action trackers, and daily stand-ups; proactively resolve blockers and risks.
Produce and/or oversee development of high-impact proposal content (management approach, staffing plans, recruiting/retention, quality assurance, transition, past performance, resumes, key personnel, etc.).
Ensure strict RFP/RFQ compliance (Section L/M, instructions/evaluation criteria), including page limits, formatting, and submission requirements (e.g., SAM.gov, eBuy, state portals).
Facilitate color-team reviews (Pink/Red/Gold or equivalent), incorporate feedback, and manage version control.
Coordinate with staffing/recruiting leadership to ensure proposed approaches align with real operational capability (credentialing, onboarding, compliance, readiness, surge staffing, rural coverage).
Build and maintain reusable proposal assets: templates, boilerplates, past performance write-ups, resumes, org charts, staffing matrices, and compliance checklists.
Support teaming activities, including partner data calls, document collection, and integration of subcontractor content.
Maintain proposal documentation and repositories; enforce quality standards and continuous improvement practices.

Required Qualifications (No Exceptions)
Minimum 5 years of proposal writing/proposal management experience supporting federal or state solicitations. (No exceptions).
Minimum 3 years exclusively focused on healthcare staffing proposals for IHS or VA or DHA (No exceptions).
Demonstrated experience managing a proposal team and leading multiple contributors under tight deadlines.
Strong command of proposal compliance, structure, and persuasive writing tailored to evaluation criteria.
Experience producing key staffing proposal sections (recruiting, retention, credentialing, transition, QA, staffing plans).
Proficiency with common proposal tools (Microsoft 365, SharePoint/Teams, Adobe/PDF tools) and disciplined file/version control.
Ability to thrive in a remote environment with high accountability, responsiveness, and attention to detail.
Must be a US Citizen

Preferred Qualifications
Experience supporting IDIQs, task orders, and multiple-award environments (federal or state).
Familiarity with healthcare staffing operational workflows (credentialing/privileging, licensure verification, background checks, onboarding, compliance tracking).
APMP certification (Foundation/Practitioner/Professional) or similar proposal training.
Experience with pricing coordination and staffing models (labor categories, rate structures, surge/coverage assumptions).

Core Competencies
Proposal leadership and deadline discipline
Compliance-first mindset and strong QA habits
Clear communication and stakeholder management
Ability to translate operations into compelling proposal narratives
Calm execution under pressure; proactive risk management
