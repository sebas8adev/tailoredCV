Job Description:
About the job
We are seeking a dynamic and detail-oriented Senior Project Manager to lead the successful delivery of medium to large-scale projects. This role is pivotal in ensuring projects are delivered on time, within budget, and to the highest standards of quality, while maintaining excellent communication and service to both internal and external stakeholders.

At ANS, we’re reshaping the digital landscape by delivering innovative experiences across diverse sectors. As a Project Manager, you’ll be instrumental in showcasing our capabilities by coordinating Power Platform and AI Hackathons that demonstrate ANS’s leadership within the Power Platform ecosystem. You’ll also lead the delivery of cutting-edge AI solutions, including modern Co-Pilot implementations that drive meaningful transformation. Additionally, you’ll oversee and execute end-to-end implementations of D365 and Power Platform projects.

Key Responsibilities

Manage project scope, quality, timelines, and budgets effectively
Approve and monitor project time tracking
Create and maintain key project documentation (RAID logs, weekly status reports, budget reports)
Lead and chair project governance meetings
Capture and document lessons learned for continuous improvement
Communicate clearly and consistently with all stakeholders
Support senior project managers on large-scale initiatives
Oversee smooth handovers to managed services post-delivery
Identify, assess, and manage project risks and issues
Collaborate with team members to ensure successful project delivery
Support Sales and Pre-Sales teams during the sales cycle
Provide cover for the Lead Project Manager when required

What We’re Looking For

Proven experience managing Dynamics 365 and/or Power Platform implementation projects
Strong communication and interpersonal skills
A customer-first mindset with a passion for delivering excellent service
A proactive problem-solver with a keen eye for detail
Experience with project governance tools and methodologies (e.g., RAID, HLRs)
Demonstrated ability to manage budgets and project scope
Highly organized and able to thrive under pressure
