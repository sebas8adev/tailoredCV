Job Summary:
ABBYY is a trusted partner for purpose-built AI and intelligent automation, helping enterprise customers transform their business processes. They are seeking a highly analytical Technical Project Manager with strong Business Analysis skills to lead IT and Information Security initiatives, ensuring timely delivery of solutions that provide measurable business value.

Responsibilities:
• Gather and analyze business, technical, and security requirements for infrastructure, risk, compliance, and digital transformation projects
• Perform impact analysis and develop business cases including cost-benefit analysis, ROI projections, and risk quantification
• Create and maintain data dashboards for project health, security posture, SLA compliance, and business impact metrics
• Translate business requirements into epics, features, and user stories using tools such as Jira or Azure DevOps
• Partner with cross-functional teams to align projects with regulatory frameworks (e.g., NIST, CIS Controls, ISO 27001)
• Collaborate with project managers and leadership to define and measure OKRs, KPIs, and performance metrics for projects and programs
• Develop dashboards and reports to visualize progress, value delivery, and compliance posture
• Identify gaps and inefficiencies through root cause and trend analysis to support continuous improvement
• Lead cross-functional project teams through the entire project lifecycle using Agile, Scrum, or hybrid methodologies
• Develop and manage project plans, scope, timelines, and budgets
• Facilitate regular stakeholder meetings, status reporting, and risk/issue tracking
• Ensure projects align with business strategy and deliver intended outcomes

Qualifications:

Required:
• Bachelor’s degree or equivalent experience in Business Administration, Computer Science, Cybersecurity, Information Systems, or a related field
• 7+ years of experience as a Business Analyst or hybrid PM/BA within IT or Information Security domains
• Strong understanding of IT operations, cybersecurity frameworks, and enterprise technology ecosystems
• Experience gathering requirements for identity and access management, cloud security, GRC platforms, infrastructure modernization, or other InfoSec-related projects
• Experience defining, tracking, and reporting on business KPIs and operational OKRs

Preferred:
• Certified Business Analysis Professional (CBAP) or PMI-PBA
• Lean Six Sigma or Process Improvement credentials
• PMP, CSM, or SAFe (a plus)

Company:
ABBYY reimagines the way people work and accelerates business by delivering the intelligence that fuels automation platforms. Founded in 1989, the company is headquartered in Milpitas, California, USA, with a team of 501-1000 employees. The company is currently Late Stage.
