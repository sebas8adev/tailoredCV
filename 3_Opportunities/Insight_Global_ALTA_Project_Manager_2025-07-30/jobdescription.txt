About the job
One of our large testing, inspection and certification services clients is currently seeking an ALTA Project Manager to join their growing team remotely who will oversee and manage large-scale land survey projects, with a strong emphasis on ALTA/NSPS Land Title Surveys supporting commercial real estate transactions. This is a full-time permanent opportunity. This role is responsible for the coordination, delivery, and quality assurance of survey products, serving as the central point of contact for clients and internal teams. The ideal candidate must be a licensed Professional Land Surveyor (PLS) and possess strong leadership and project management experience in survey operations and real estate due diligence. Responsibilities include:
Act as the lead project manager on ALTA/NSPS Land Title Survey portfolios delivering timely and accurate execution of deliverables in compliance with client expectations and industry standards.
Serve as the main client liaison for assigned survey programs, providing regular updates, addressing inquiries, and managing client relationships.
Direct the planning, resourcing, and coordination of multiple concurrent survey projects from inception through delivery.
Collaborate cross-functionally with land surveyors, project managers, zoning specialists, and administrative staff to drive program execution.
Conduct detailed technical reviews of ALTA survey deliverables to ensure compliance with current ALTA/NSPS standards, state regulations, and internal quality guidelines.
Maintain strict adherence to program budgets, schedules, and performance metrics while identifying areas for improvement and cost control.
Onboard new clients and survey programs in partnership with operations and sales teams, developing customized scope and service strategies.
Mentor and lead junior staff and survey coordinators, providing leadership and technical guidance to support team development and program growth.
Monitor changes in Kentucky survey laws, ALTA requirements, and industry trends to ensure continued compliance and excellence in service.
