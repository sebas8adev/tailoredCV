About the job
A Snapshot of Your Day

The Project Manager will lead cradle-to-grave project management oversight of the engineering, manufacturing, delivery and installation of upgraded components for Canadian Nuclear Generator Projects and other complex projects. Generator upgraded equipment includes stators, rotors, excitation equipment, auxiliaries, and other balance of plant systems.

Serve as primary connection with external customers and internal stakeholders (including marketing, engineering, district service offices, factories, order management, finance, etc.).
Establish effective communication with partners regarding regular project updates, design reviews, factory testing, contract deliverables, and witness points.
Maintain and control project financials including emergent contract issues, change orders, etc. Develop Plans to deliver Quality and Risk Management for the project.
Ensure engineering and hardware deliverables are complete in line with Canadian Nuclear Standards (N299) and customer technical specifications.
Work with customer procurement partner to document hardware Quality Testing and Inspection results. Implement Zero-Harm directives for overall project safety performance.


What You Bring

Bachelor's Degree in Engineering or related, or combined equivalent work/education background. PMI or IPMA Certification is a bonus; but if not yet completed, must successfully complete IPMA certification within three years of starting.
5+ years experience in customer relationship management and previous project management or field service experience demonstrating capabilities in implementing large, complex high-value projects (with concentration on generators preferred).
These projects typically contain appreciable and complex design engineering content; proficient understanding of overall design, installation, and operational complexities of generators, excitation equipment, and auxiliary systems is preferred (steam and combustion turbine knowledge is a bonus).
Able to gain unescorted access to nuclear power plants.
Required travel 15% including internationally!
