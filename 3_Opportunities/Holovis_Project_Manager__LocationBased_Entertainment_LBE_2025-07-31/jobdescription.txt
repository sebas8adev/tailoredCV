Job Description:
About the job
Your Mission
Step into the spotlight and help bring world-class immersive experiences to life. As a Project Manager at Holovis, you’ll lead the delivery of cutting-edge location-based entertainment (LBE) projects across the globe—on time, on budget, and to the highest quality standards.

 What You’ll Do
Own the Project Lifecycle: Manage multi-million-dollar AV and software installations for theme parks and immersive venues from concept to completion.
Lead Global Deployments: Travel internationally for extended periods to oversee on-site delivery and client engagement.
Drive Collaboration: Liaise with clients, creatives, and technical teams at all levels—translating vision into reality.
Keep It on Track: Scope, schedule, budget, and resource your projects using tools like MS Project, Smartsheet, and JIRA.
Manage the Matrix: Lead cross-functional teams with clarity, empathy, and accountability.

 What You Bring
Proven experience managing complex LBE or themed entertainment projects end-to-end.
Strong grasp of AV systems, interactive software, mobile apps, or game development.
Certifications like PMP, PRINCE2, or ScrumMaster are a big plus.
Comfortable working across time zones and cultures—international team experience is a bonus.
Willingness and ability to travel globally for extended deployments.

 Bonus Points
You’re a natural communicator—able to deliver tough messages with tact.
You thrive in fast-paced, creative environments.
You’re passionate about immersive storytelling and emerging tech.

 Additional Info
You must have the right to live and work in the USA.
This role is based in Orlando, Florida, and requires regular presence at the Holovis office, and at sites globally
