Overview And Essential Job Functions

The Scrum Master / Delivery Lead is responsible for driving delivery with urgency while keeping the team grounded in quality, feedback, and iteration. They will own sprint rhythms, AQ coordination, release schedules, and stakeholder transparency.

 Lead all Agile ceremonies: standups, sprint planning, retros, demos 
 Own QA, testing coordination, and change management 
 Monitor progress and remove impediments to delivery 
 Serve as the glue between technical and non-technical stakeholders 
 Track metrics (velocity, throughput, adoption) and drive continuous improvement 
 Other duties as assigned 
 ALL Acumen employees will be vigilant to support the positive compliant cybersecurity company posture by familiarizing themselves with all policies, procedures, standards, and guidelines and act accordingly. 

Qualifications

MINIMUM QUALIFICATIONS

 4+ years in Agile delivery, project management, or scrum master roles 
 Strong facilitation and conflict resolution skills 
 Calm under pressure, outcomes-focused, highly organized 
 Experience delivering data or automation projects in an Agile environment 

Preferred Qualifications

 Scrum certification (CSM, SAFe, or equivalent) preferred 
 Background in BI/analytics or automation preferred
