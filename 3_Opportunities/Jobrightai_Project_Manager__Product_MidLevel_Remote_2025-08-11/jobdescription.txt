Job Summary:
Baylor Genetics is a company focused on innovative genetic testing solutions, and they are seeking a Project Manager - Product to lead their projects. The role involves managing project artifacts, coordinating with cross-functional teams, and ensuring projects align with company objectives while maintaining high-quality standards.

Responsibilities:
• Responsible and accountable for project artifacts such as the Project Plan, Project Charter, Project Kickoff, Project Midterm, Project Closeout and Lessons Learned.
• Lead the initiation, planning, execution, monitoring and closeout of your assigned program projects, ensuring alignment with company objectives.
• Develop and maintain detailed project plans, timelines, and budgets.
• Identify project resources and allocate them effectively to meet project goals.
• Coordinate with cross-functional teams to assign tasks, manage resources, and ensure project deliverables are on schedule.
• Work closely with internal stakeholders to ensure smooth project execution.
• Collaborate with external vendors or partners to ensure the delivery of digital projects on time.
• Communicate project goals, updates, and outcomes to senior management and stakeholders.
• Proactively identify potential risks or issues that may affect the project schedule, budget, or scope.
• Lead problem-solving efforts to ensure the resolution of project roadblocks in a timely manner.
• Regularly review and assess project performance, adjusting plans as needed to stay on track.
• Monitor the progress of digital projects to ensure high-quality standards are met throughout the process.
• Perform regular project audits to ensure adherence to established quality, compliance, and regulatory standards.
• Prepare and deliver detailed reports to senior leadership regarding project status, risks, and achievements.
• Manage and set clear expectations for stakeholders regarding project timelines, deliverables, and potential risks.
• Foster strong relationships with stakeholders to ensure their needs are understood and addressed throughout the project lifecycle.
• Provide leadership and guidance to team members and other stakeholders.
• Continuously assess and improve program project management processes to increase efficiency and ensure the best outcomes.
• Develop and maintain best practices for program project management.
• Performs other job-related duties as assigned.

Qualifications:

Required:
• Bachelor’s degree in life sciences, biotechnology, healthcare administration, or a related field.
• PMP or other project management-related certifications are highly advantageous.
• 4+ years of experience in project management, with a focus on projects with some technical aspect(s) and at least 2 years in the laboratory or healthcare space.
• Ability to simultaneously manage multiple projects of moderate complexity.
• Extensive experience in managing cross-functional teams.
• Experience working with external vendors or partners is a plus.
• Excellent communication, organizational, and leadership skills.
• Effective interpersonal, negotiation and meeting facilitation skills.
• Demonstrated ability to lead teams through ambiguous situations.
• Ability to collaborate effectively with stakeholders at all levels.
• Experience applying both Agile and Waterfall methodologies.
• Proficiency in project management software (Jira is a plus).
• Strong problem-solving skills with the ability to identify and address project risks and issues.
• Strong attention to detail and accuracy.
• Passion for continuous improvement.

Company:
Baylor Genetics offers a full spectrum of cost-effective, genetic testing, and provides clinically relevant solutions. Founded in 1978, the company is headquartered in Houston, Texas, USA, with a team of 201-500 employees. The company is currently Growth Stage.
