🧠 About the Role

We are seeking an experienced Project Manager with a background in real estate development, design coordination, and permitting/entitlements. This individual will lead and manage the approval and execution process for infrastructure and site development projects from pre-construction through permitting.

The ideal candidate brings a blend of project execution skills, regulatory experience, and the ability to collaborate across engineering, legal, and government stakeholders. This role requires minimal supervision and a proactive mindset.

🛠️ What You’ll Do

Manage the end-to-end permitting and entitlement process for infrastructure development projects
Collaborate with design engineers, permitting consultants, and local/state agencies
Track project milestones, budgets, and deadlines using MS Project, Excel, and Smartsheet
Coordinate across multiple stakeholders to maintain schedule and regulatory compliance
Provide regular updates and reports to senior leadership
Drive strategic execution while managing daily details of complex permitting workflows

✅ Key Requirements

5–7 years of experience in project or program management related to real estate, infrastructure, land development, or permitting
Strong knowledge of entitlements, zoning, site planning, and design coordination
Highly proficient in Microsoft Excel and MS Project; familiarity with Smartsheet
Experience working with cross-functional teams including design, legal, and permitting agencies
Able to work independently with minimal direction
Bachelor's degree in engineering, planning, construction management, or a related field
