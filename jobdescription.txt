About the job
Job Description: 

Optomi, in partnership with our client, is seeking a skilled Scrum Master to support a high-performing team focused on building innovative data assets. This role will lead Agile ceremonies, champion Scrum best practices, and foster a culture of continuous improvement across a data-centric environment.


Responsibilities:

Facilitate all core Scrum ceremonies including sprint planning, daily stand-ups, sprint reviews, and retrospectives.
Guide the team in Agile best practices, focusing on Scrum and Kanban to drive continuous delivery and team self-organization.
Proactively remove impediments to team progress, encouraging collaboration and problem-solving.
Promote a high-performing team culture grounded in openness, transparency, and accountability.
Provide coaching and mentorship on Agile methodologies and team development.
Act as a liaison between teams and stakeholders, ensuring alignment on goals and expectations.
Collaborate with the Product Manager to maintain and prioritize a clear and actionable product backlog.
Support effective conflict resolution strategies to enhance team cohesion.
Champion a learning culture by encouraging skill development and knowledge sharing related to Agile and modern data technologies.


Must Haves:

Proven experience as a Scrum Master or Agile Coach, ideally in data or analytics-driven environments.
Strong understanding of Scrum principles and broader Agile frameworks such as Lean or XP.
Experience with product ownership and data management concepts including governance, quality, and lifecycle.
Familiarity with DevOps practices and tools, contributing to CI/CD and data pipeline automation.
Proficiency in Agile tools, particularly JIRA.
Background working in data environments with tools/platforms like AWS and Snowflake.
Excellent facilitation, communication, and coaching skills.
Strong problem-solving abilities and comfort navigating conflict resolution.
Ability to promote transparency, innovation, and team accountability.


Preferred Qualifications:

Certified Scrum Master or Agile certification (CSM, PSM, SAFe, etc.).
